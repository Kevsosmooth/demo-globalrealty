Global Realty Development is a full-service property management and real estate development firm operating across New York City.

Our Story

Redefining NYC
Real Estate

Founded with a commitment to providing quality housing across the Bronx and greater New York City, Global Realty Development has grown into a trusted name in property management and real estate development.

We believe that every tenant deserves a well-maintained, professionally managed home. Our team works around the clock to ensure that every property in our portfolio meets the highest standards of quality and comfort.

From single-unit studios to multi-family developments, we handle every aspect of the rental process with precision and care.
